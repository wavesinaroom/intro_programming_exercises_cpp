4.    Explain the concept of exceptions and exception handling, when they are used and how to catch exceptions.

5.    Explain when the statement try-finally is used. Explain the relationship between the statements try-finally and using.

6.    Explain the advantages when using exceptions.

-------
Answers
-------

4. Exceptions are ways to control the flow of a crashing program by providing a detailed 
report to the user.

5. Try statements executes the code within and redirects the flow if an error happens.
Finally statements wrap the actions that are executed in spite of having an error in the program.

6. Exceptions are extremely helpful for debugging code. On the other hand they aid end users
to determine causes for program crashes during their experience. Essentially, exceptions
are powerful for improving software in general.
